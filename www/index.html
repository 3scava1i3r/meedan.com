Meedan.org builds social technologies
to increase information diversity.

We work with researchers, institutions, funders, and partners in the world on a range of cutting-edge projects in journalism and education. We have a focus on multilingual technology and the Arabic-speaking region. Take a look at some our recent projects:

  • Checkdesk
  • Classroom to Classroom
  • Nurani
  • Translatedesk
  • Arab Citizen Media

On the Blog — June 2014

In 2014 we continue our work on open code, open data, and open values with a stellar set of supporters through Meedan.org. And we will also work on sustaining this vision — and our close knit community of engineers, designers, translators, and idealists — by introducing Meedan Labs. We are building market-changing products for the cross-lingual internet. Follow our progress on the Meedan blog.
