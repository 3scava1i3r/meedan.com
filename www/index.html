Meedan
n.
[mēˈdan] Arabic
1. A town square
2. A gathering place

Meedan.org is a non-profit working with cross-lingual and collaborative global networks to create new opportunities for journalists and educators.

We do so with the hope that in improving the information diversity of our global online town square we are also improving our world.

On the Blog — June 2014

Meedan’s digital media verification toolkit Checkdesk has been selected amongst the winners of the prestigious Knight News Challenge, winning Prototype Fund support to fund research, development and testing of new features.

Journalist Josh Stearns, who runs the popular Verification Junkie web site, recently called the project “one of the most well thought out and holistic verification tools I had seen created for newsrooms.”

Follow our progress on the Meedan blog.
