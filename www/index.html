On the Blog — June 2014

Meedan’s digital media verification toolkit Checkdesk has been awarded multi-year funding by the Swedish International Development Cooperation Agency (Sida) to support independent online journalism in the Arab world.

Journalist Josh Stearns, who runs the popular Verification Junkie web site, recently called the project “one of the most well thought out and holistic verification tools I had seen created for newsrooms.”

Follow our progress on the Meedan blog.
