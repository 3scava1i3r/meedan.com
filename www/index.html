Meedan

Make sense of the global web.

We are designers, technologists and journalists building digital tools for collaboration on the global internet, with a focus on fact verification, open journalism, and social translation. With university and commercial partners we support research, curriculum development, and new forms of digital storytelling.

Meedan has offices in Cairo, Vancouver and San Francisco, with a distributed team across 13 time zones, 6 cities, and 3 continents. Collectively, we speak 8 languages and want to learn a lot more.

Contact Us

Meedan is a hybrid nonprofit/for-profit organization. We share code, people, offices, and values across two companies. Meedan.org is a registered 501(c) 3 established in 2006 that works on non-commercial projects to support global journalism. Meedan Labs is a for-profit spun off in 2014 to develop software for crowdsourcing social media translation.
