Our projects and products support open-source investigations, digital media verification, media literacy training and long-term research into indicators of information quality.

We are a group of technologists, journalists, researchers and designers. We collaborate on building solutions to complex and uncharted global challenges on the web.

Town Square

Meedan is Arabic for “town square,” a gathering place that fosters connection, dialogue and understanding. We envision global information systems that are reliable, safe and equitable for all.
