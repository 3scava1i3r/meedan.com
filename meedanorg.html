Meedan builds tools and digital gathering places to connect cross-cultural communities.
We have a vision of a cross-lingual web.

At Meedan we think that the more we diversify the information we use to make sense of this big, complex, and diverse planet, the better we will understand our place in it. Meedan has been working to improve the linguistic and cultural diversity of our digital information diet since 2006.
