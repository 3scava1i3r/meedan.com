Meedan.org is a non-profit organization designing and building social technologies to increase the information diversity of our global town square.

Meedan is the Arabic word for “town square.”

We have been honored to work with some of the greatest researchers, institutions, funders, and partners in the world on a range of cutting-edge projects in journalism and education, with a focus on the Arab region. Take a look at some our recent projects:

  • Checkdesk
  • Classroom to Classroom
  • Nurani
  • Translatedesk & Out of Eden Walk

Funders and Sustainability

We have been honored to work with some of the greatest researchers, institutions, funders, and partners in the world on a range of cutting-edge projects in journalism and education, with a focus on the Arab region.

In 2014 we will continue our work on open code, open data, and open values with a stellar set of supporters through Meedan.org. And we will also work on sustaining this vision - and our close knit community of engineers, designers, translators, and idealists - by building market changing products for the cross-lingual internet through our spin-off Meedan Labs.
